How do you process online donations?

We use Stripe, which is one of the world’s largest online payment processing platforms. All donations are processed through their secure server. Neither us, nor them will ever share any of your details with third parties.

Can I donate without a credit card?

Yes, we support other payment methods. Please get in touch with us at donate@ourworldindata.org.

How can I manage my monthly donation?

To change or cancel your monthly donation, please get in touch with us at donate@ourworldindata.org.

Where are donations sent to?

Our World in Data is a collaborative effort between researchers of the Oxford Martin Programme on Global Development at the University of Oxford, who are the scientific editors of the website content; and the non-profit organization Global Change Data Lab, who publishes and maintains the website and the underlying data engine.

Gifts made to Our World in Data through the donations page go to the Global Change Data Lab.

Further questions

If you have any further questions regarding donations, please get in touch with us at donate@ourworldindata.org.
