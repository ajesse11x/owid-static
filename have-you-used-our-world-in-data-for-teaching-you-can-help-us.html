Have you used Our World in Data for teaching? You can help us!

by Esteban Ortiz-Ospina
July 04, 2018

We want to understand how educators use our work. If you have ever used our work for teaching, at any educational level, we would love to hear from you.

We have set up a survey with only 5 short questions. We will use your answers to make our work more useful. We won’t collect your email – your answer will be anonymous.
